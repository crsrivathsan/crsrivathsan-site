Background

The Full Picture

I'm a Product Manager who sits at the intersection of deep technical fluency and customer-obsessed design. My foundation is network engineering, giving me a grasp of infrastructure, latency, and system constraints that lets me make faster, better decisions when building AI-powered products that run at carrier scale.

At Verizon, I've led the shift from static IVR decision trees to intent-driven Agentic AI using Google Customer Engagement Suite, integrated GenAI across chatbot and RCS channels, and shipped novel UX primitives like In-Bot Video Containers that required co-ordinating across AI, network, and product teams.

Before Verizon, I ran product at Tata Elxsi, launching an award-winning V2X solution, managing 5G NFV/ETSI infrastructure products, and building Go-To-Market strategies that closed marquee enterprise deals.
